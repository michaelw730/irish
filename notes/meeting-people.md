## Faisnéis | Information

The words mé and tú are called pronouns and are used when you don't need to or want to repeat a noun. Here is a list of pronouns, accompanied by the present tense of the verb bí (be).

* Tá mé (I am)
* Tá tú (you are)
* Tá sé/sí (he/she is)
* Tá muid (we are)
* Tá sibh (you are)
* Tá siad (they are)
