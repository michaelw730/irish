TRADITIONAL TOASTS 

Sláinte mhaith agat i bhfad ó bhrón 
Good health to you, free from sorrow. 

Sláinte do bheo agus trócaire do mharbh. 
Health to the living and mercy on the dead. 

Go mba fada a mhairfidh tú. 
Long life to you. 

The word duitse means ‘for you’ or ‘to you’ and is made up of the 
following: 

do (for / to) + tú (you) + se (which is used to add emphasis to tú). 

Earlier we saw the word domhsa, which means ‘for me’ or ‘to me’ 
and is a combination of the following elements: 
do (for / to) + mé (me) + sa (which is used to add emphasis to mé).
