3.  This is my grandmother and my grandfather. 

4.  This is my mother and my Uncle Jeff.

INTRODUCING YOURSELF AND OTHERS 

This is how you introduce yourself and others collectively: 

Is muidne*… 
Is muidne deirfiúracha John. 
Is muidne deartháireacha Laura.  We’re Laura’s brothers. 

We’re … 
We’re John’s sisters. 

* In Ulster Irish this word usually has three syllables in everyday speech:  
muid-in-ne.

3.   You’ll  be  asked  where  various  people  are  now  and  also  where 

they work. Give the appropriate answer in each case. 

1.  —Cá bhfuil Marion agus John anois? 

Say, ‘They live in Belfast.‘ 

—Agus cá bhfuil siad ag obair? 

Say, ‘Marion works in a shoe shop and John is unemployed at the 

moment.‘ 

2.  —Cá bhfuil Séamas Holmes anois? 

Say, ‘He lives in Dublin and he works in a supermarket.‘ 

3.  —An bhfuil Vera ina cónaí in Aontroim anois? 

Say, ‘No, she lives in Enniskillen.‘ 

—Agus cad é an post atá aici? 

Say, ‘She works in a restaurant.‘ 

4.  Tú féin! 
Can you now say where various members of your family live and where they work?
